# Privacy Policy for Drawr

**Last updated:** January 2026

## Data Collection

Drawr does **not** collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All data stays on your device.

## What Drawr Stores Locally

- **Drawings:** Your annotations are saved locally in Chrome's storage so they persist when you revisit a page
- **Keyboard shortcuts:** Your customized keybindings are stored locally

This data never leaves your browser and is not accessible to us or any third party.

## Permissions Explained

- **activeTab:** To inject the drawing canvas on the current page
- **scripting:** To add the drawing tools to webpages
- **storage:** To save your drawings and settings locally
- **host_permissions:** To capture screenshots

## Third-Party Services

Drawr does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or external services.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
